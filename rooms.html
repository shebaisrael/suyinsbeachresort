Double

Located on the third floor of the Main Wing, Alta Vista rooms are suitable for guests who want to enjoy peace and quiet surroundings as the rooms are secluded from the poolside and beachfront activities.

Rooms can accommodate two adults and two children below seven years old.

FEATURES:

  • Each room measures about 25 sqm
  • With 2 single beds
  • Mountain view
  • Bathroom with one sink, rain shower and premium toiletries

Personal Care:

  • Algotherm toiletries

Entertainment:

  • TV with in-house movie channels
  • Wireless internet access

Refreshments:

  • Welcome drink on arrival
  • Bottled water
  • Mini-refrigerator
