To reach Suyin's Beach Resort in Almeria, Biliran:


By Air:


Fly to Daniel Z. Romualdez Airport in Tacloban. From Tacloban, take a van or bus to Naval, Biliran.



By Sea:


Take a ferry from Cebu to Naval, Biliran. From Naval, take a tricycle or van to Almeria.



By Land:


If coming from Leyte, take a bus or van from Ormoc to Naval. From Naval, proceed to Almeria via tricycle or van. Once in Almeria, locals can guide you to Suyin's Beach Resort, which is situated near the beachfront. Safe travels!
